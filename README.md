Collect Seashells!

Expect gameplay to be around 20-30 minutes long (per ending, 2 endings total).

Submission for Cozy Jam: Summer 2026

This is my first game jam, so it'd be great to give me some critiques or criticisms to work on for the future!

There may be updates in the future, depending on how much time I have on my hands.

<in-game intro>

You're Sam, Sally's brother, and she needs you to help her collect some seashells for her seashell business! Collect seashells along the shore, and get 300 seashells to kickstart her business! 

You can also use your seashells to buy upgrades from the conveniently placed seashell upgrade shop that manipulates the waters to aid you in your seashell collecting journey!

However, whatever you do, DO NOT COLLECT SEA URCHINS!! They will take away your seashells, even ones you don't have yet! Something bad may happen if you dive too deep into those waters...

On an unrelated note, if you collect more seashells, the sea urchins you end up collecting seem to run away from you...

<end>



If you couldn't tell already, more content lies in collecting enough sea urchins to make Sally leave you.
